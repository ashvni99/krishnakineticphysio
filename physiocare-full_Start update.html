✦ Explore Medical Department

Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy solutions covering every aspect of physical health and rehabilitation

🦴
Shoulder Pain

Shoulder pain physiotherapy focuses on the assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of conditions affecting the shoulder muscles, joints, tendons, and nerves. It helps reduce pain, improve mobility, restore strength, and support recovery from injuries, stiffness, posture-related problems, and everyday strain.

🏋️
Therapeutic Exercises

Therapeutic exercises are specially designed physical activities that help improve strength, flexibility, balance, posture, and overall body movement. These exercises support recovery from pain, injury, surgery, and muscle weakness while promoting long-term physical health and mobility.

🦴
Hip Pain

Hip pain physiotherapy focuses on the assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of conditions affecting the hip joints, muscles, ligaments, and surrounding tissues. It helps reduce pain, improve flexibility and movement, restore strength, and support recovery from injury, arthritis, muscle strain, and posture-related problems.

🦵
Knee Pain

Knee pain physiotherapy focuses on the assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of conditions affecting the knee joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons. It helps reduce pain, improve mobility, restore strength, and support recovery from injuries, arthritis, sports strain, and everyday movement problems.

🧍
Back Pain

Back pain physiotherapy focuses on the assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of conditions affecting the spine, muscles, joints, and nerves of the back. It helps reduce pain, improve posture and flexibility, restore movement, and support recovery from muscle strain, poor posture, injury, and long-term back problems.

🦶
Foot & Ankle Pain

Foot and ankle pain physiotherapy focuses on the assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of conditions affecting the foot, ankle joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons. It helps reduce pain, improve balance and mobility, restore strength, and support recovery from injuries, sprains, heel pain, and movement-related problems..

🧠
Neurological Physiotherapy

Neurological physiotherapy focuses on the assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. It helps improve movement, balance, coordination, strength, and independence in individuals recovering from stroke, paralysis, nerve injuries, Parkinson’s disease, and other neurological conditions.
